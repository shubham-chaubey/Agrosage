About AgroSage 2.0

Built for India's farmers — combining AI, RAG, and bilingual support to make expert crop guidance accessible to everyone.

AgroSage 2.0 is an AI-powered crop recommendation and query system designed to support farmers with smart, data-driven decisions. Our mission is to make agriculture more accessible, efficient, and informed — especially in rural communities of Uttar Pradesh and across India.

🌾

Smart Crop Suggestions

Tailored ML-based predictions using NPK, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall data.

🧠

RAG Query Engine

Ask any farming question and get instant, context-aware answers via Gemini 2.5 Flash.

📊

Agricultural Database

Backed by curated Indian agricultural datasets with crop metadata, seasons, and soil types.

🌐

Bilingual Support

Responds fluently in Hindi, English, and Bhojpuri — breaking language barriers for rural farmers.

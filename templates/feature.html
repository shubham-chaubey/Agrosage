What We Offer

Features That Empower Farmers

AgroSage combines machine learning, retrieval-augmented generation, and bilingual support into one powerful platform.

🤖

AI Crop Recommendations

Smart ML predictions based on soil NPK values, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall — tailored for Bharat's diverse climate zones.

📚

RAG-Based Q&A

Ask any farming question. Our RAG pipeline retrieves relevant context from the agricultural database and generates precise answers via Gemini AI.

🌐

Multilingual Support

Fully bilingual in Hindi and English. Understands Bhojpuri too — designed for farmers across Uttar Pradesh and rural India.

🧠

Conversational Memory

AgroSage remembers the last 6 turns of your conversation for smarter, context-aware replies without repeating yourself.

📊

Confidence Score

Every crop recommendation includes a confidence percentage from the ML model so you can trust and verify the output.

🏃

Fast & Lightweight

Optimized Flask backend with AJAX-based chat — works well even on slower rural internet connections.

🔬

Disease Detection (Soon)

Upcoming CNN-based image recognition to detect plant diseases from crop photos and recommend treatments.

📋

Feedback System

Farmers can submit questions and suggestions directly — continuously improving AgroSage's knowledge and responses.
